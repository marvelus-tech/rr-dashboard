# Kyle's Key Insights — The Koerner Office Ep. #296 + rentlocalsites.com

## The Business Model
**Website Landlord / Rank & Rent**
1. Build website for niche + city (e.g., "junk removal Austin")
2. Do SEO to rank on Google page one
3. Install tracking phone number
4. Forward calls to local business owner
5. Charge flat monthly fee for leads

## Kyle's Numbers
- Monthly revenue: ~$104K
- Personal profit: ~$35K/month
- Overhead: <$5K/month
- Per site cost: ~$25/month
- Work: 1-2 hours/day
- Client retention: 5+ years average
- First client: 6 years ago, still paying

## The "Free Trial" Pitch (80/20 Close)
> "Hey man, I got this website getting 50 calls a month for [niche] in [city]. I'm not a [niche] company. Can you take them for a week?"

- No credit card, no contract
- 1 week free
- Close rate: 1 in 3 free trials converts
- After trial: negotiate flat monthly fee

## Pricing Strategy
**What Kyle Tried (Abandoned):**
- ❌ Pay-per-call — unpredictable, causes anxiety
- ❌ Tiered pricing — clients question every lead
- ❌ Commission-based — requires trust to verify

**What Kyle Uses Now:**
- ✅ Flat monthly fee ($300-$5,000/month)
- ✅ No contracts — cancel anytime
- ✅ Undercharge on purpose

> "I don't want people to feel [the bill] every month and get punched in the gut. I'd rather make less and have this be 100% passive."
