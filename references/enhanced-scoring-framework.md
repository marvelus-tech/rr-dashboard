## The Kyle Scoring Framework (Enhanced)

| Factor | Weight | What to Look For |
|--------|--------|------------------|
| Lead Value | 2.5x | Avg job value × close rate. $500+ minimum. $2,000+ ideal. |
| Low Competition | 2.0x | Inverted score. KD under 30. Weak incumbents (DA < 35). No franchise saturation. |
| Traffic | 1.0x | 40+ monthly searches for primary [service] + [city]. Long-tail cluster potential. |
| Urgency | 1.5x | Emergency/insurance-driven = higher conversion. Seasonal = riskier. |

**Composite Score = (Lead Value × 2.5) + ((11 − Competition) × 2.0) + (Traffic × 1.0) + (Urgency × 1.5)**

## 🗺️ LOCATION PAIRING STRATEGY

Best City Profiles:
1. **Mid-size suburbs (50K–200K population)** — enough volume, weak incumbents
2. **Growth corridors** — new housing = new service demand, low DA competition
3. **Satellite cities near major metros** — e.g., Ballarat (near Melbourne), Geelong, Werribee
4. **Avoid**: CBDs of major cities (Sydney, Melbourne CBD) — franchise saturation

Example Winning Combos:
- Foundation Repair → Werribee VIC (growth corridor, pre-1990 housing stock)
- Mommy Makeover → Geelong VIC or Frankston VIC (affluent demographics, weak medspa SEO)
- Bed Bug Removal → Dandenong VIC or any dense rental suburb
- Fall Proofing → Mornington Peninsula VIC or Gold Coast QLD (retiree density)
- Stump Grinding → Any leafy suburb with mature trees + new development
- Solar Bird Proofing → Suburbs with high solar penetration — check solar install maps

## 📋 VALIDATION CHECKLIST (Before You Build)

- [ ] Search `[service] + [city]` — Are incumbents DA < 35 with thin content?
- [ ] Map Pack check — Is it dominated by 3 real businesses, or directory spam?
- [ ] Lead value test — Can one closed job cover 3–6 months of rent?
- [ ] Recurring potential — Does the service have natural repeat demand?
- [ ] GBP feasibility — Can you legally/physically verify a profile? (Avoid locksmith trap)
- [ ] Urgency signal — Does the search imply "need it now"? (Emergency > scheduled)
- [ ] Franchise scan — Are Jim's, Flick, Rentokil, or Servpro owning the SERP?
- [ ] Domain availability — Is [service][city].com.au or similar available?

## 🚀 RECOMMENDED BUILD ORDER

**Month 1**: Launch 2× Tier 1 sites in different niches but same region (e.g., Ballarat Asbestos + Ballarat Foundation Repair) to share citation building and local backlink economy.

**Month 2**: Add 1× Tier 1 + 1× Tier 2 recurring niche (Commercial Pest Control or Cleaning) to test subscription rental pricing.

**Month 3**: Scale the winner. If Foundation Repair Ballarat rents at $4,500/mo, replicate in Bendigo, Shepparton, and Warrnambool.

**Never build Tier 3 as your first site.** You will burn 6 months and conclude "R&R doesn't work" when the niche was just wrong.
